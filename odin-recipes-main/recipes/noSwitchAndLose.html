“Fårikål”


Ingredients:


  1. 2 kg Lamb meat on the bone (cut in 3 cm thick slices)
  2. 1,5-2 kg Cabbage
  3. 2 tbsp Butter
  4. 4 tsp Black pepper grains
  5. 2 tbsp Flour
  6. 400 ml water
  7. Salt

Instructions:


  1. Cut the cabbage in four and remove the hard part in the center. Cut each part in further 3 pieces lengthwise
  2. Add the butter in small pieces in a big casserole.
  3. Start with a layer of meat, then cabbage, meat, cabbage…
  4. Drizzle the flour, pepper grains and salt in between each layer.
  5. Bring the water to boil and pour it over the layers.
  6. Cover and bring to boil. Let it simmer on low heat for 3 hours.
  7. It is important not to stir! If you stir your cabbage will decompose.
  8. The meat should be so tender that it easily falls off the bone. If it is not, you need to cook it a bit longer…
